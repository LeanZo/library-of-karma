### **Description:**

*Eko Eko Azarak: Wizard of Darkness* is a Japanese horror film released in 1995, directed by Shimako Satō. This dark and supernatural thriller is based on a popular manga and anime series. It blends elements of horror, occultism, and psychological suspense to create a chilling and mystifying narrative.

### **Summary:**

In *Eko Eko Azarak: Wizard of Darkness*, high school student Misa Kuroi arrives at a new school, but she possesses a dark secret - she is a powerful witch. As mysterious and gruesome events begin to occur at the school, Misa finds herself drawn into a malevolent supernatural force. She must use her supernatural abilities to confront a malevolent cult and the dark forces they serve.
The film builds on the legacy of the "Eko Eko Azarak" series, offering an atmospheric and suspenseful narrative that combines elements of horror and the occult. *Eko Eko Azarak: Wizard of Darkness* continues the captivating story of Misa Kuroi, the powerful witch, and her battle against the forces of darkness.
